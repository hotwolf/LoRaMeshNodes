Here is new stationary solar node [SSNvD](https://github.com/hotwolf/LoRaMeshNodes/tree/main/SSNvD).
It's a compact version of the [SSNvA](https://github.com/hotwolf/LoRaMeshNodes/tree/main/SSNvA).
The antenna holder and the solar panel mount can be swapped by the SSNvA ones.
